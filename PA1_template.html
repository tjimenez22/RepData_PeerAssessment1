Are there differences in activity patterns between weekdays and weekends?

From this graph we can see that the mean of steps per interval is almost the same in the first 500 intervals, between intervals 500 and 1000 people move a little bit more on weekdays, and for the next intervals (till interval 2000) people are moving more on weekends. I can said that the patterns are different from weekdays and weekend.

library("timeDate")
library("lattice")
newData$day<-ifelse(isWeekday(newData$date),"weekday","weekend")

names(results)[1]<-"day"
names(results)[2]<-"interval"
